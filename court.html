CCS Mobile Legends tournament

The Buglasan Festival, also known as “The Festival of Festivals,” is a highly anticipated event held annually in Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental, every October. The festival honors the province’s heritage, originating from the term "Buglas Insulis" depicted in a 1572 map by Diego Lope Provedano. It was created to unite the people of Negros Oriental, showcasing the rich culture and traditions from the northern town of Canlaon City to the southern part of Bayawan City...
