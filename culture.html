Culture

Silliman University is a vibrant hub of culture that celebrates the diverse heritage of its community while promoting creativity and inclusivity. Through its various cultural programs, performances, and festivals, the university showcases the richness of Filipino traditions alongside global influences. The institution fosters a sense of pride in local artistry, from music and dance to visual arts and literature, encouraging students and faculty to express their identity and explore new ideas. Cultural events like the Hibalag Festival and artistic exhibitions provide platforms for collaboration and dialogue, emphasizing the importance of cultural appreciation in building a compassionate and open-minded society. At Silliman, culture is not merely preserved but continually reimagined, enriching the educational experience and inspiring individuals to connect with their roots while embracing the world.

CCS Acquaintance Party

On September 28, 2024, our college held an acquaintance party with the theme "Mafia." The event took place at the elementary grounds and was designed to help students and faculty build connections in a fun and interactive setting. My role during the event was to serve food, ensuring that attendees were well-fed and satisfied throughout the celebration. I worked alongside others like staff from the cafeteria and my other committee members. Participating in this event taught me the importance of teamwork and attention to detail in event organization. I initially expected the party to be just another formal gathering, but the themed activities and interactions made it much more exciting and memorable. I was particularly surprised by people wearing costumes like batman and joker. One individual who left a strong impression on me was Franz when he was hosting. His energy and heart that he put into making sure the participants were having fun made the experience more meaningful. While the most satisfying part of my role was seeing attendees enjoy the food and camaraderie, the most challenging part was the amount of people as many tables who haven’t been called stood up to get food and we had to explain to them to wait their turn. This activity highlighted my values of hospitality and service as I contributed to the event's success. This experience reinforced the importance of creating spaces where people can connect and have fun. I learned how to manage a line better, and I plan to apply this by helping out in future events. I would love to stay involved in organizing and participating in similar events, as they help strengthen relationships and build a sense of belonging. The benefits of this activity were clear, it fostered a lively and inclusive environment for everyone. If I were to do this again, I would plan ahead to streamline service and engage more with attendees because it is an acquaintance party after all. Because of this activity, I am more aware of how small acts of service can contribute to the joy and success of community events.
