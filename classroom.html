Classroom

Silliman University emphasizes the value of the classroom as a space not just for academic learning but for holistic development. It is a place where intellectual curiosity is nurtured, and students are encouraged to think critically, engage in meaningful discussions, and approach problems with a creative and ethical mindset. Silliman fosters an environment that upholds academic excellence, integrity, and respect for diversity, promoting a collaborative and inclusive atmosphere. The classroom experience at Silliman is designed to empower students to become well rounded individuals, equipped with both the knowledge and character to contribute positively to society. This commitment to quality education goes hand in hand with the university's mission to develop leaders who are not only skilled professionals but also responsible global citizens.

Honda Road Safety Seminar

On August 20, 2024, I attended the Honda BeAT Road Safety on Campus event, held at the Audio Visual Theater and hosted by Honda. The event aimed to raise awareness about road safety among students and professors, particularly focusing on safe practices for motorcycle riders. As a participant, I observed presentations and demonstrations that highlighted good road safety procedures, practical tips, and the importance of adhering to safety regulations. This event made me more aware of the critical need for road safety, especially on campus, where motorcycles are a primary mode of transportation for many students. I expected the session to focus on general traffic rules, but I was surprised by its relevance to real-life situations and the common challenges faced by riders in our community. One of the most striking aspects of the session was how it addressed frequent misconceptions about road safety, making the lessons more practical and relatable. The presenters effectively illustrated how small decisions, like wearing the right gear or following traffic signals, can make a significant difference in preventing accidents. The most impactful part of the event was the interactive demonstrations, which underscored the importance of vigilance and responsibility on the road. This seminar reinforced the importance of practicing and promoting road safety in our daily lives. It inspired me to be more cautious and mindful of my actions as a rider while also encouraging others to prioritize safety. Moving forward, I plan to consistently apply the lessons learned, such as conducting pre-ride checks and adhering to traffic rules, and share these practices with peers who ride motorcycles. The event also highlighted the need for ongoing education on road safety, especially in environments like universities where young riders are common. Because of this activity, I am more committed to ensuring road safety for myself and others and motivated to take an active role in promoting awareness among my peers.
